What We Do

Three ways we get students building

Not a lecture series. Every pillar below ends with something a student actually shipped.

Hackathons

From idea to a working demo in one day. CodeForge put 122 builders through an "AI for Productivity" sprint with mentors on the floor and a live judging panel at the end.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions that pick up where the syllabus stops. ML Arena taught regression through classification on a real Kaggle stock-price dataset, not a slide deck.

Inter-college Events

We don't just attend tech fests — we run them. AI-VERSE brought students from across Bengaluru together under one AI & ML banner, guest of honor included.
