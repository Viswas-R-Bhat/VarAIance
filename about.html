About

Variance, with AI hiding inside it

"VarAInce" reads as variance — the statistical measure of how far a set of values spreads from its mean — with AI embedded in the middle. It's the kind of name that only makes sense once you've taken a statistics course and an AI course in the same semester, which is exactly the position most of our members are in.

Origin Story

Born with the department, in 2022

VarAInce didn't precede the AI & DS department and didn't follow it either — they started in the same year, which is part of why the club has always felt like an extension of the coursework rather than a break from it.

2022

Department and club, together

BMSCE opens its Artificial Intelligence & Data Science department. VarAInce forms alongside it as the department's student club — a place to apply what's taught in lecture before the next semester's syllabus catches up.

2025

First flagship events

CodeForge Hackathon and ML Arena run back-to-back on April 4 — a hackathon judged by industry leaders and a hands-on ML workshop co-run with the ISE Student Club.

2026

Going inter-college

VarAInce conducts AI-VERSE, an inter-college fest for AI & ML innovation, with Dr. Indiramma M as guest of honor — the club's first event built for an audience beyond BMSCE.

Mission

Bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world AI

Lecture covers the math behind a model. We try to cover everything lecture doesn't have time for — picking a dataset, fighting with preprocessing, watching a model fail, and shipping something anyway in the time it takes to run a hackathon. The goal isn't to replace the AI & DS curriculum. It's to give it somewhere to land.
